Pluto's Story

Synposis

The adventures of a timid duo, pessimistic alien named Pluto and his mischievous partner in crime Charon in the Milky Way Galaxy. A place where Venus is a beautiful desert planet and Saturn is a lavish cloud planet filled with luxurious cities. A place where the planets are ruled by an egotistical conquering alien race known as the planeterians who possess the ability to manipulate gravitational forces. The two travel from planet to regain their spot in the Milky Way Alliance and dispel the notion that the lowest tier of their race's hierarchy system can't amount to nothing.
