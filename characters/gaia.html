Gaia

Gaia

She is a much like Neptune as she doesn't want to be bothered with the planeterian policies. She tends to separate from it. Gaia spends most of her time on Gaia watching her subjects which she sees more of as children living their lives. She acts like an overprotective mother to them and for the longest time, she shielded them from the rest of the Milky Way Sector.

Gaia is also into the arts and aesthetics. She runs an art blog on Tumblr and Instagram. She posts aesthetic images she takes on her visits to Terran towns and cities.
