Neptune

Neptune

Neptune is not like that other celestial grade planeterian. He has the personality of a dwarf planeterian. has reserved, shy, timid, soft-spoken. He doesn't talk to the others with Uranus and Pluto being the exception. Neptune is socially awkward. He's very close to his children and they are the only people that see and talk to on a daily basis. He doesn't really care for the other planeterians as he sees them as heartless sobers. Being around them brings out his negative traits such has his harsh anger and sadistic nature. He would have been kicked out of the milk way alliance if it wasn't for his immense strength a power.
