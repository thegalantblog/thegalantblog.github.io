Minerva

She is incredibly smart and calculating. Minerva has a very dry and sarcastic sense of humor. During a majority of her life she was overtaken with the thirst of vengeance. And because of this she tends to be cares in battle when she gets pissed off. Minerva was the tactician behind the dwarf planeterian rebellion. She believes the hierarchy is extremely flawed. However, after an encounter with Pluto, she decides vengeance and violence isn't going to make things better.

Minerva loves to wear sweaters and read books about space and the planeterian race. Steamy hot tea is one of the few things that can lighten her mood and warm up her cold heart. Her favorite animal is an owl. Her more was studying birds in college before see came to Jupiter. She passed down her love for them to her daughter to put all the things that make her happy in one place.
